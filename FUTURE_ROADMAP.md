# AI Spend Audit – Future Roadmap

## Overview

This document outlines potential future improvements and expansion opportunities for AI Spend Audit.

The current version focuses on:

* lightweight AI spend auditing,
* recommendation generation,
* and public report sharing.

Future iterations could evolve the platform into a more comprehensive:

* AI operations,
* procurement,
* and spend optimization system.
