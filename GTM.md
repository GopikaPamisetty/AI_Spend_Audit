# AI Spend Audit – Go-To-Market Strategy

## Overview

This document outlines the go-to-market (GTM) strategy for AI Spend Audit.

The product is positioned as:

* a lightweight AI cost optimization platform,
* focused on helping teams reduce unnecessary AI tooling expenses.

The initial strategy prioritizes:

* rapid distribution,
* organic visibility,
* and developer-focused adoption.

---

# Product Positioning

## Core Positioning

AI Spend Audit helps teams:

```text id="gtm1"

```

The messaging focuses on:

* cost savings,
* operational visibility,
* and subscription optimization.
